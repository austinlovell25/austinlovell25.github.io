Research

Undergraduate Research in SKILL Lab — Purdue University
Researcher (Sep 2023 - May 2025)

Researched and implemented computer vision methods using PyTorch to track human facial movements during speech for improved diagnosis of sensorimotor issues. Achieved tracking accuracy sub 1 millimeter error with novel methods. Advised by Prof. Raymond Yeh and Prof. Kwang S. Kim at Purdue University. First authored a paper with results from this research, which is currently under review for submission to IEEE Transactions on Neural Systems & Rehabilitation Engineering.
